PENDER GUITAR STUDIO

Connect With Your Inner Musician

What Sets Us Apart

Pender Guitar Studio is a guitar & music lesson studio founded by Stu Pender that is focused on helping all types of students connect with their unique inner musician.

"My passion is in helping students find & nurture their inner musician. Whether you are preparing for a performance, looking to expand your musical horizons, or just seeking expression through music — there is a place here for you."

  1. Training from one of the world’s top music conservatories

    2. Stu is an active performer, musician & composer who graduated from the Jazz Conservatory at the State University of New York at Purchase College.

    He's had the honor of studying with some of the great musicians of our time including John Abercrombie, Jon Faddis, Vic Juris, Charles Blenzig, Barry Harris, Fabiano do Nascimento, Derek Gripper, & Miguel Atwood-Ferguson.

  2. Experience working with students widely spanning in age & skill level

    3. Over the past decade, Stu has worked with a large body of students spanning a variety of ages and levels of experience. While finishing his Bachelor's Degree, he taught at the State University of New York at Purchase College as the professor of an accredited course for non-major music students. He’s also taught New York City Public Elementary School after-school music programs, and now primarily teaches as a music instructor through his private studio, Pender Guitar Studio.


    Aside from his own, some methodologies that Stu loves to teach include:

    • Derek Gripper's Montessori Method for Guitar (Beginner)
    • The Julio Sagreras Guitar Lesson Books (Beginner to Advanced)
    • David Leisner's Playing With Ease (All Students)
    • Kenny Werner's Effortless Mastery (All Students)
    • The jazz pedagogy of his teachers John Abercrombie, Vic Juris, Charles Blenzig, & Barry Harris (Intermediate to Advanced)

  3. An extensive resume of performing and recording music

    4. Stu Pender is a guitarist and composer from the suburbs of Chicago, IL. After performing at a young age with blues rock band Los Lobos and later studying with ECM pioneer John Abercrombie, Stu settled in Brooklyn, NY where he’s performed or recorded with artists such as Photay, Elijah Wolf, Poetic Thrust, Maggie Rogers, Raphaelle, Kareem Ali, Yoh, Allison Ponthier, Topaz Jones, and the Gipsy Kings — at world class venues including the Beacon Theatre, United Nations Assembly Hall, MoMA PS1, Brooklyn Steel, and many more.

    Stu has also written & performed music for advertisement & film, most recently playing guitar on the soundtrack of the Sundance Film Festival award-winning short film "Don't Go Tellin' Your Mama".

    Stu continues his study in the traditions of Brazilian guitar & the Kora music of Mali. Working in a wide variety of styles has led him to carve out a sonic signature uniquely his own. His upcoming solo record Stillness In Motion will be released in early 2022 by Youngbloods Records.


    Find more on Stu's Personal Website & Instagram

What You'll Learn

Pender Guitar Studio uses a teacher guided goal setting & reflection system (stored for each student in the cloud using Notion) with a focus on achieving a long-term visualized outcome through weekly practice assignments, teacher feedback, and regular progress check-ins. These monthly, weekly, and daily logs are kept fun, flexible, and in the service of the student's interest and growth.

The following categories when paired with long-term goals give stucture to each student's personal curriculum.

  1. Listening & Ear Training
  2. Music Theory & Technique
  3. Repertoire & Reading
  4. Writing & Recording

Pricing

15 Minute Lesson — $15

This is a private Zoom check-in.

Designed for students who are very self motivated and on a budget, the 15 minute check-in is the most affordable option. You will receive feedback on your playing and new practice material weekly.

30 Minute Lesson — $30

This is a condensed private Zoom lesson.

Reccomended for younger students & for those who like to go over material more quickly, the half hour lesson is an affordable way to dive more deeply into your musical goals. You will receive feedback on your playing and new practice material weekly.

60 Minute Lesson — $60

This is a full length private Zoom lesson.

Designed for students who want to dive deeply into material each week with the guidance of a teacher, the hour lesson is the most robust option towards achieving your musical goals. You will recieve feedback on your playing and new practice material weekly.
