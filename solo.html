Stillness in Motion is an album from Stu Pender first sparked by a stay in Big Sur. The songs were completed during the Spring of 2020 in Brooklyn, NY as a way to reconnect with that deep natural refuge. Weaving guitar, voice, field recordings, and dreamed melodies, Stillness In Motion is a reflection on how important it is to pause and take time from this feeling of busyness, for simple things that bring joy. Stillness in Motion was released on digital platforms worldwide Thursday January 27 by Youngbloods.

stillnessbubbles is a collection of mostly loop-based compositions on the guitar. Recorded & self-released in November 2018.
